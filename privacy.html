Privacy Policy - Quick Site Shortcuts

Data Collection

Quick Site Shortcuts respects your privacy. We collect only the following information:

  • URLs and titles of your custom shortcuts (stored locally on your device)
  • License key information for premium users (stored locally on your device)

Your shortcuts and settings are stored using Chrome's storage sync API, which means they are synced across your devices when you are signed into Chrome.

License Validation

For premium users, we validate your license key with our licensing server. During this validation:

  • Your license key is transmitted securely to verify its validity
  • No personal information is collected during this process
  • License validation only occurs when you activate, deactivate, or refresh your license

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your devices using Chrome's storage APIs:

  • Custom shortcuts (URL, title, domain, and command ID)
  • License information for premium users
  • User preferences and settings

We do not have access to this data as it is stored locally on your device.
